Where to Find Equipment

The boards and bags are stored in the shed near the fire pit. Please return everything there when finished.

Setup & Rules

Place the boards facing each other. Traditional distance is 27 feet from front edge to front edge, but mountain-house rules allow “close enough.” Play to 21.
